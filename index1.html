COMPANY PROFILE


BACKGROUND

RAYVILL ELECTRICAL CONSTRUCTION CORPORATION was registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission in May 1993. It is principally engaged in providing service to Manila Electric Company (MERALCO) and its customers in its day-to-day operations. The relationships of the owners of the company with MERALCO and its customers, however predates the incorporation date. The Chairman of the company, Raymundo D. Villanueva Jr. is the son of Raymundo V. Villanueva Sr., the President of Villanueva Builders and Electrical Construction Corporation (VIBECCO) which was established in 1978. The family members actively participated in the management and operations of the said corporation. The company employees always maintain harmonious relationships with MERALCO personnel as well as continued courtesy to all its customers.

After the death of the President of VIBECCO, the corporation was dissolved and the family members created their own businesses, all duly accredited with MERALCO. These are Powerline Construction Inc., ERV Electrical and Rayvill Electrical Construction Corporation. All these companies continue to coordinate and share in technical know-how, financial resources and equipment in order to adequately service MERALCO and its customers with a good quality of performance.

The Company has steadily built its reputation and industry leadership within the, MERALCO contractors through dedication to quality, partnership not only with our clients but also with our employees and a constant desire to grow as a company. In 23 years the company has built substantial expertise in the electrical construction industry.

The Company has now begun expansion into different but related industries that match up with our core competency. Rayvill has been a part of government projects like the construction of the Manggahan Floodway thru partnership with DAEWOO Construction of Korea and OCDC of Japan. The Company was also involved with the hauling of aggregates for the construction of the Hanjin – Redondo Shipyard and has now just finished the construction of the first residential house in White Plains Subdivision.

Since 1993, the Company has served the electrical construction & service needs of residential, industrial, institutional and commercial communities throughout the greater Manila and Bulacan areas. Through the years, the company has developed and maintained a solid reputation for honesty, ability, dedication and friendliness. As such, it has gained a tremendous amount of knowledge and experience making its works meticulously completed in a timely fashion with pride, trust, and value ultimately resulting in total customer satisfaction.
